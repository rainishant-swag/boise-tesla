⚠ Critical Gap — US-95 Corridor

The US-95 corridor from Lewiston, ID to Meridian (Boise area) has zero Tesla Superchargers for 81 miles through White Bird Canyon. This is a remote, mountainous stretch with no urban charging infrastructure.


Day 1: Always charge to FULL at Lewiston SC before heading south on US-95. Arrive Meridian with ~199 mi — comfortable.

Day 4: Charge fully at Boise SC before departing north. You'll have 280 mi when you leave — the 154 miles to Lewiston will leave you at ~126 mi, so the Lewiston SC stop is mandatory on the return trip.
